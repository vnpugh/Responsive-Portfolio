About Me

Greetings! My name is Velvet Pugh and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer. Prior to making a career change into web development, I worked as an auditor and International Tax Accountant with various companies, including KPMG, Citibank and Catholic Health Initiatives. In addition, I graduated from Florida State University with a double major in accounting and labor economics. I am also a local member of Women Who Code, She’s Coding and a volunteer for Dallas Independent School District. Also, I am originally from Tampa, FL, but I’ve lived across the U.S. including Atlanta, New Orleans, Chicago, Denver and now Dallas, TX.


When I am not spending time with my family or working, I love to travel, attend music events and take weekend road trips. One of the perks of growing up in Florida is that it is easy to travel to the Caribbean Islands by ferry or plane. One of favorite islands to visit with my family is the Bahamas. During the holidays, we travel by ferry from Fort Lauderdale to Nassau to enjoy good music and endless amounts of rum. Overall, I am excited about working as a web developer and improving my skill set in HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript and MySQL. Additionally, I am looking forward to learning Angular and other tech skills in the future including Python, Node.js, jQuery, React and MongoDB.
